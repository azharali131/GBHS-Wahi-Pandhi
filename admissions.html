Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's admission at GBHS Wahi Pandhi.

01

Visit the School

Come to GBHS Wahi Pandhi campus during working hours (Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM).

02

Get Admission Form

Collect the admission form from the school office. Our friendly staff will guide you through the process.

03

Fill & Submit

Fill out the form completely and submit it to the office along with required documents.

04

Confirmation

After verification, your child's admission will be confirmed. Welcome to GBHS Wahi Pandhi! 🎉

Required Documents

Please bring these documents at the time of admission.

B-Form (Child's Birth Certificate)
Previous School Leaving Certificate
Last Year's Result Card
Parent/Guardian CNIC Copy
2 Passport Size Photographs
Residence Proof (Electricity/Gas Bill)
